All our product pages, policies and support information in one place.

1. About Our Products

All microgreens and sprouts are grown in a hygienic, controlled environment using food-grade materials and quality-tested seeds. As microgreens are fresh and perishable, natural variations in size, color, and growth are normal.

All orders depend on daily harvest availability. Orders may be modified or canceled if the product is out of stock or not harvest-ready. ORVYN reserves the right to limit product quantities per customer.

3. Pricing

Prices shown at purchase time are final. Prices may change without prior notice due to seasonal variations, seed cost fluctuations, or production factors. Discounts are valid only for their mentioned duration.

4. Payments

We accept UPI, online payments, and cash on delivery (for select locations). Payments must be completed before dispatch unless COD is selected.

5. Delivery & Pickup

Delivery timelines vary depending on location and harvest schedules. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before the daily cut-off time. Delivery delays caused by traffic, weather, or external issues are not the responsibility of ORVYN.

As microgreens are perishable, we follow a strict no-return policy. Replacements are offered only for damaged, spoiled, or wrong products delivered.
